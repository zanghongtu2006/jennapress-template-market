Jenna Press already supports the core needs of a focused project website: multilingual pages, a blog with simple first-level categories, static-friendly deployment, and browser-side persistence for theme and language.

What is still partial is mostly editorial convenience and advanced content modeling. The framework is not yet trying to cover docs portals, product catalogs, search-heavy content systems, or visual editing workflows.

That is intentional. The current support level is strongest where the project keeps its boundaries small and explicit.
