  <h3>Description</h3>
  <p>SaaS Landing is a polished, conversion-focused landing page template built for technology companies. It features a dark neon aesthetic with high-contrast surfaces that look great on all screen sizes.</p>

  <p>The template supports all major JennaPress content blocks including hero sections, feature grids, stats displays, CTA banners, rich text, and contact forms. It is fully multilingual (English and Chinese) and ships with three themes: dark, pink, and light.</p>

  <h3>How to use this template</h3>
